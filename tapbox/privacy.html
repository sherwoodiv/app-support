Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

Tapbox ("the App") is a storage box inventory app for iPhone and iPad that helps you catalogue and find the contents of your storage containers.

Data Collection

Tapbox does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The App has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Camera & Photos

The App requests access to your camera to let you photograph box contents. Photos are processed and stored entirely on your device. They are never uploaded, shared with third parties, or sent over the network.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

The App requests microphone access to record voice memos describing box contents, and speech recognition access to transcribe those memos for search. Voice recordings and transcriptions are processed on your device and are never sent to external servers.

NFC

The App uses NFC to read and write tags on your storage containers. NFC data is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted externally.

Subscriptions

Tapbox offers an optional subscription managed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. The App does not process or store any payment information. All transaction data is handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data Storage

All data — including boxes, photos, voice memos, and transcriptions — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact
