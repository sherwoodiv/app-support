Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Pooka Collage ("the App") is a free photo collage maker for iPad, designed for kids and families.

Data Collection

Pooka Collage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Photos & Camera

The App requests access to your photo library and camera solely to let you add photos to your collage. Photos are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded, shared with third parties, or sent over the network.

Data Storage

All data — including saved collage projects and photo selections — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the share or save feature to export a finished collage.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Pooka Collage is designed to be safe for children. The App does not collect any information from any user of any age. No personal data, usage data, or device identifiers are gathered.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue.
