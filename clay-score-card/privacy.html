Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 28, 2026

Clay Score Card ("the App") is a clay shooting score tracker for iOS.

Data Collection

Clay Score Card does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

Data Storage

All data — including shooter names, scores, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only, using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the share feature to export a score card image.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional tip jar purchases processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. No payment information is collected or handled by the App. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. No data is collected from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue.
